The Hydra is a DHARMA Initiative zoological research station located on the Hydra Island, a few miles offshore of the the main island.

The Arrow is DHARMA Initiative station number 2, whose primary purpose is the development of defensive strategies against the Hostiles.

This station is designed to be a laboratory to study and manipulate the "unique electromagnetic fluctuations emanating from the Island"

The Orchid station is dedicated to exotic matter research, namely time travel, under the guise of being a botanical research station.
